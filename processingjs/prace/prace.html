PRace

Controls: Keep the left mouse button pressed to gain thrust, release button to fall.
Gameplay: Survive as long as possible, i.e., avoid crashing into enemies or walls.
Hints:

  • Flying close to enemies gives higher scores per second (indicated by white line).
  • The required minimum distance for higher score (negatively) depends on the size of the enemy, i.e., it is easier with larger enemies.
  • Green enemies are very small, and flying close to them gives twice the score of blue ones.
  • Slightly overlapping with enemies given even higher score (cyan line), but beware: you die when your core touches the enemy (red line).
